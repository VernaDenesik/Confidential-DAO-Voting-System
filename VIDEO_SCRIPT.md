## Technical Requirements

### Video Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio:** 128 kbps AAC stereo
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9

### Recording Tools
- **Screen Capture:** OBS Studio or Camtasia
- **Video Editing:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Voiceover Recording:** Professional microphone with noise cancellation
- **Graphics:** After Effects for animations or Canva for static graphics

### Visual Style
- **Color Scheme:**
  - Primary: Blue gradient (#4A90E2 to #1E3A8A)
  - Accent: Purple (#7C3AED)
  - Success: Green (#10B981)
  - Warning: Amber (#F59E0B)
- **Typography:**
  - Headlines: Poppins Bold
  - Body: Inter Regular
  - Code: JetBrains Mono
- **Animation:** Smooth transitions (0.3s ease-in-out)
- **Branding:** Consistent with Zama visual guidelines

### Audio Guidelines
- Clear, professional voiceover
- Background music: Subtle tech/corporate track at 20% volume
- No distracting sound effects
- Balanced audio levels throughout

### Accessibility
- Include closed captions (SRT file)
- High contrast text for readability
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Avoid flashing or rapid scene changes
